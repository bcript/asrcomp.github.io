Singapore Olympiad in Informatics (SOI)

National Competition

The premier competitive programming competition in Singapore. Top performers may represent Singapore at the International Olympiad in Informatics (IOI).

  • Open to all JC students
  • Focus on algorithms and problem-solving
  • Training provided for interested students
  • Held annually around March-May

National Olympiad in Informatics (NOI)

National Competition

A challenging programming competition that tests algorithmic thinking and implementation skills across multiple rounds.

  • Multiple competition categories
  • Excellent preparation for university-level CS
  • Recognition from universities and employers

International Bebras Challenge

International Competition

Test your computational thinking skills through engaging logic puzzles and problem-solving challenges.

  • Accessible to all skill levels
  • Develops logical reasoning
  • Certificates for top performers

Build-On Singapore Hackathons

üí∞ CSIT Scholarship Programme

The Computing & Information Technology (CSIT) Scholarship is awarded by the Centre for Strategic Infocomm Technologies to nurture Singapore's cybersecurity and infocomm technology talent.

What's Covered

  • Full tuition fees
  • Maintenance allowance
  • Pre-studies allowance
  • Return airfare (for overseas studies)
  • Accommodation allowance (for overseas studies)
  • Computer allowance

Eligibility Criteria

  • Singapore Citizen
  • Good academic records
  • Strong interest in computing and technology
  • Leadership qualities and co-curricular activities
  • Accepted into approved university programmes

Career Prospects

  • Work with CSIT upon graduation
  • Cybersecurity and ICT roles
  • Cutting-edge technology projects
  • National security contributions
  • Continuous learning and development

Application Process

Applications typically open in February each year. Students should prepare their A-Level results, personal statements, and undergo interviews. Having a strong H2 Computing portfolio and participation in computing competitions can strengthen your application.

Learn more: Visit the official CSIT website at www.csit.gov.sg
